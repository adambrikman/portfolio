About Me

Hey! I'm Adam

I was born and raised in Las Vegas, NV. I graduated in 2015 with a Masters in Accounting. Shortly after graduating, I passed the CPA exams and began working at a top public accounting firm.

While working at the firm, I stumbled across CS50, the free introductory CS course provided by Harvard Extension school.

After completing (and struggling!) through the first assigment in the C language I realized I just spent a week of my spare time on 'homework' for enjoyment.

It was then and there I decided I would complete course, no matter what.

Fast-forward to now

CS50 changed my life because it changed the way I approached problem solving. It was through the challenges in the course that I fell in love with programming.

I couldn't believe there was a career where I could build things, have critical thinking included in my work life and enjoy what I was doing. I began looking up what it would take to become a software engineer.

I've since expanded my skills to delve further into responsive web design as well as both front-end and back-end development.

When I'm not programming, I'm traveling, exercising or eating.
